## Project Mandate & Overview

The strategic goal was to establish the website as a robust **lead generation tool** while functioning as a comprehensive, digital service catalog.

### Core Objectives
* **Lead Generation:** Facilitate direct customer inquiries by prioritizing **"Book a FREE site survey and quote"** calls-to-action.
* **Service Display:** Clearly detail the full service catalog and end-to-end process to establish credibility.

### Key Features Implemented
* **Customer Conversion:** Multiple Quote Request Forms across the site for efficient lead capture.
* **Service Process:** Detailed step-by-step guidance on the end-to-end service, from site survey to pump installation and water check.
* **Service Catalog:** Comprehensive sections covering Precision Borewell Drilling, Pumpset Services, Pipeline Works and Legal/NOC Assistance.
* **Proof & Trust:** Dedicated sections for Customer Reviews and easily accessible contact information.

## Custom Execution & Strategy

This project involved a **Custom-build approach**, necessitating meticulous precision to meet stringent client requirements.

* **Design Mandate:** Execution was driven by a **meticulous** design brief provided by the client, requiring the exact implementation of non-standard layouts and custom aesthetics.
* **Technical Execution:** The entire site was built **from scratch** on the Wix platform to precisely translate the client's complex vision. Successfully integrated required animations and custom interactions.
* **Cross-Device Optimization:** Focused heavily on **pixel-perfect responsiveness**, meticulously developing both **mobile and desktop versions** to ensure a premium user experience across all devices.
